Fix 2 — Payment modal warning: Tyler enters $250, approvedAmount is $300

When "Save & Complete" is clicked with a paymentAmount ≠ approvedAmount, the job log modal closes and this dialog replaces it. Tyler must explicitly choose — no auto-save.

⚠ Payment ≠ Job Price
Payment $250 differs from job price $300.

Update Job Price + Record Payment — if the job was actually $250. Updates the price everywhere (jobHistory, lifetimeSpend) and records $250 collected.

Record Payment Only — if collecting a different amount than the agreed price (partial payment, discount after the fact). Job price stays $300, $250 logged as collected.
Edge case: no matching jobHistory entry (job not yet written)
If saveFullEdit finds no matching jobHistory entry for the completed job (shouldn't happen for normal calendar completions, could happen for legacy data), it logs a warning to the console and still recomputes lifetimeSpend from existing entries — no silent skip. Tyler won't see it unless DevTools is open, but it won't corrupt data.
