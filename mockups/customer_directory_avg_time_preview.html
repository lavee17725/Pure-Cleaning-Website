Customer Directory — Avg Time/Worker Column

New column between "Last Service" and "Jobs". Four display states covered.

PRE-COMMIT PREVIEW
New column: "Avg Time/Worker" between Last Service and Jobs.
Sort key: numeric _avgTPW (minutes) — missing data sorts to bottom.
Display format: "Last: 1h 21m" when n=1 matched job · "Avg: 1h 33m" when n≥2 · "—" when no Bouncie data.
Computation: bouncieMetrics[address].avgMinPerWorker — pre-cached on customer record by Phase 3. Aggregated across properties for multi-property customers.
Mobile: column hidden at ≤700px via .col-avg-time{display:none}. Full table hides at ≤640px (existing behavior). Card view gets avg time in .cust-card-avg sub-line.
