MATH FIX
Bug (commit d9626d3): perWorkerMinutes = actualDuration ÷ DEFAULT_CREW_COUNT — divided when it should multiply. A 93-min job with 2 crew showed 47 min (half the job time), not 186 min (what Tyler actually pays for).

Fix: laborMinutes = actualDuration × DEFAULT_CREW_COUNT — labor hours = time × people. A 93-min job with 2 crew = 186 labor-minutes (3h 6m) billed.
