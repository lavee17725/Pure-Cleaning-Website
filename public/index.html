Common Questions

How much does it cost?

Every property is different, so we quote each one — free, fast, and with no obligation. Most quotes are ready the same day. Tap "Get Your Free Quote" and you'll have a number before you know it.

How often should I have my home cleaned in South Florida?

With our heat and humidity, floors — driveways, patios, pool decks — do best every 6 months, and roofs every 2 years. We'll tell you honestly what yours needs — we've been looking at South Florida homes since 1995.

Is soft wash right for my roof?

Shingle roofs and Spanish S-tile (Venezuelan tile) are always soft washed — it's the right treatment for them, period. Soft wash also pairs especially well with homes that have gutters, since they channel the rinse-down cleanly. No gutters? No problem — we plan the job accordingly and protect everything below.

Is it safe for my plants and pets?

Yes. We protect landscaping on every job and use treatments appropriate to each surface. Thirty years, thousands of homes — we treat every property like it's ours.

Do I need to be home?

No — as long as we can access the exterior, we handle everything and follow up with you when the job's done.
