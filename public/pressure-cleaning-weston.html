The job around here

Weston is our biggest market — and it has been for 30 years. It was actually our first: we lived in Weston ourselves before we moved to Southwest Ranches. Many of our Weston customers are repeat clients who've stayed loyal for years — some for multiple decades — across Indian Trace, Bonaventure, Weston Hills, and the communities around Sawgrass.

If you live in Weston, you know the HOAs stay on top of how your roof and driveway look — and a dirty roof or a stained driveway can mean a letter in the mailbox. We take care of that: your home back to looking its best, and the HOA notice off your back.

Weston has every kind of roof, and we service all of them the right way — whether yours calls for a soft wash or pressure washing, we've dealt with it all and we know how to take care of whatever you've got. Beyond roofs, we clean driveways, patios, pool decks, and fences, and rinse down all the walls and windows of the house — whatever the property needs.

For pavers, two things matter over time. When the joints open up and the sand washes out, we re-sand them — which also helps suppress the weeds and ants that work their way through. And when the color starts to fade, a seal brings back that rich, wet-look finish and protects the surface for years. After 30 years in South Florida, we know the right approach for each home.

If you're new here or you've been around forever and just never bothered to call anybody, the quote is free and there's no rush. Get a same-day quote or call (954) 389-2642 and Tyler will pick up.

Common questions

Do you cover all of Weston?

Yes — every zip code in the city: 33326, 33327, 33331, 33332. We work Indian Trace, Bonaventure, Weston Hills, the Sawgrass-adjacent communities, and the smaller HOA-managed streets in between.

Do you work with HOAs?

Often. Many of the HOAs in this area require pressure cleaning every 1-2 years, and we coordinate timing with their inspections. We can quote one home or several houses on the same street.

How much does pressure cleaning cost?

Every property's different — roof size, driveway material, what's on it. Quotes are free and usually back the same day. Start your quote here.

How quickly can you get out?

In summer — our slower stretch — we can usually get a residential job on the schedule within one to two weeks, sometimes sooner. If it's time-sensitive (a real estate listing, an HOA inspection, a closing), tell us when you call and we'll work around your date.

Is it cheaper at certain times of year?

A little — yes. Summer is our slower season, so there's more room on the calendar and a bit more flexibility on price. If you can plan ahead, that's the time to book. From about October through March it's our busy season — demand is higher, pricing firms up, and we can be booked out a few weeks, so the earlier you reach out the better.
