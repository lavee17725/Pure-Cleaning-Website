Roof cleaning
Select your roof cleaning method *
⚠️ We do not service metal roofs — too dangerous for our crew.
Softwash ⭐ Premium

Low-pressure application with professional-grade cleaning solution. Works by breaking down algae, mold, and buildup at the source for longer lasting results.

✓ Pros
Faster cleaning process · Kills algae & mold at the root · Longer lasting results
⚠ Consider if...
Home has no gutters · Gutter downspouts drain near sensitive plants · Yard has delicate landscaping — chemicals can run off into soil
📋 Required for: Shingle roofs · Spanish S tile (Venezuelan/Altusa tile) — these roof types must always be cleaned with softwash to protect the material.
Traditional brush cleaning

Manual brush cleaning with soap and water. A thorough, effective clean — takes a bit longer but gentler on surrounding landscaping.

✓ Pros
Less aggressive on plants & soil · Better choice for homes without gutters · Limited chemical runoff · Good for sensitive landscaping
− Cons
Takes longer · Results may not be as dramatic as softwash
Water only pressure cleaning

Pressure cleaning with water only — no soap or chemical solution. Think of it like washing your hair with just water. You'll still get a solid clean and remove the majority of surface dirt and buildup, just without the deep treatment that soap provides.

✓ Pros
Zero chemical exposure · Safe for all landscaping · Same great results on surface dirt and buildup
− Cons
Won't fully remove stains, algae, or dark streaks · Results will be noticeably less dramatic · Growth returns faster
